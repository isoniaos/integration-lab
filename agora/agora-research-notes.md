# Agora Research Notes

## Practical Prerequisites

- Public Agora documentation or integration requirements for the current
  research question.
- Candidate governance contract or client surface if compatibility is being
  evaluated.
- Public Agora URL when a relevant record exists.

## Research Workflow

1. Record the research question.
2. Link the relevant Agora documentation, governance page, or integration note.
3. Identify required contract, event, API, or client surfaces.
4. Label any gaps or unsupported assumptions.

## Trust Boundary

Agora records are research, context, or links in this lab. They are not
IsoniaOS governance authority unless a scoped integration defines that authority
boundary in product documentation and implementation.

## Managed Execution Smoke Usage

For the managed execution smoke scenario, keep Agora in the research lane unless
an explicit compatible governor or provider path is validated. Agora links can
be recorded as research context, not as proof of IsoniaOS approval or execution.
